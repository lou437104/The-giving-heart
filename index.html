Why are we holding a summer charity ball

The SEN department is holding a summer charity ball because as some of you are aware Cancer has affected numerous members of staff and their families in this school, it is especially close to the SEN departments heart at the moment.

to this, the SEN department have decided to put on a Charity Ball (Black Tie and Posh Dresses) in the Summer Term to raise awareness and hopefully some money for the two following charities:

  • Teenage Cancer Trust
  • St Giles Hospice

About the charities

Teenage Cancer Trust is dedicated to improving the lives of young people with cancer, providing them with specialized care and support.

St Giles Hospice offers care and support to people with life-limiting illnesses, helping them to live well and die with dignity.
